Paintings

Painting is a visual art form that involves applying paint, pigment, color, or other mediums to a solid surface such as canvas, wood, or paper.
It is both the act of creating and the finished work itself, capturing ideas, emotions, and aesthetic qualities in a two-dimensional visual language.

Ceramics

Small ceramics, often handcrafted, are valued for their unique characteristics that reflect the artist's touch.
These pieces frequently exhibit features like subtle glaze textures, uneven shapes, pinholes, black spots from the clay's natural minerals, and fine cracks called "crazing."
These elements add character and indicate the handmade nature of the ceramics, distinguishing them from mass-produced items.
